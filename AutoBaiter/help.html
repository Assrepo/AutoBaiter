How to Use

  • First of all go to Whitelist Tab and Add all your current followings to the Whitelist. It might take a while depending on your number of followings
  • Now open a user where you want to grab followers from, Once you open the page a button ["Grab Followers"] will be added to the page.
  • Once you click it, AutoBaiter will start collecting users from them.
  • Now you may enable follow and unfollow. Autobaiter will automatically follow random users and unfollow them after specific number of days you have defined in the settings
